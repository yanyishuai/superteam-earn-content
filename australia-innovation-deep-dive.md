## 1. Why Australia for digital economy startups?

**Structural advantages:**
- **Timezone bridge** — overlaps APAC morning + US West Coast evening for global teams
- **Regulatory clarity trajectory** — digital asset frameworks evolving faster than 2022–2024 freeze
- **Deep tech + fintech talent** — universities + Big4-adjacent fintech alumni pool
- **Stable banking rails** — AUD on/off ramps complement USDC global settlement on Solana

**Superteam Australia role:** Accelerates founders across payments, digital assets, AI, infrastructure, consumer apps, and ICM — not just hackathon tourists.

## 3. Investment & capital competitiveness

**Competing with Singapore/Hong Kong:**
- **Cost of living / burn** — Sydney/Melbourne cheaper than SG for early teams (varies by stage)
- **Grant density** — Superteam bounties + ecosystem grants reduce pre-seed dilution
- **US capital access** — Australian founders still fly Colosseum / SF corridor; Solana Foundation network is global

**ICM thesis:** On-chain cap tables, tokenized SAFEs, and Streamflow-style vesting mean Australian startups can **raise and operate globally from day one** without waiting for local VC maturity.

**Friction points:**
- Legacy bank de-risking for crypto businesses
- Talent visa / remote hiring for specialized Rust engineers
- Consumer crypto adoption still niche vs TradFi superannuation culture
