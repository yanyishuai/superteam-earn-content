**2/** **The game loop (30 seconds):**  
- Circular board with **30 tiles** (Zinc's atomic number — clever)  
- Pick 1–30 tiles, deploy SOL behind each (min ~0.001 SOL)  
- Round closes → one winning tile is drawn  
- Winners split SOL from losing tiles + freshly minted **$ZINC**

**3/** **Why encryption matters:**  
On normal chains, bots copy winning wallets and front-run. ZINC encrypts your tile selections **client-side** before they hit chain. Arcium's MXEs compute over ciphertext — nobody sees your picks until resolution. Strategy stays private. MEV stays out.

**4/** **Arcium stack inside ZINC:**

Team can't predict or influence outcomes. That's the trust model.

**5/** **Payout mechanics:**  
Winners on the selected tile receive:  
- Pro-rata share of SOL on the **29 losing tiles**

Winnings accrue to your on-chain profile — claim manually (10% claim fee redistributes to active miners).

**6/** **Special rounds:**
